About Pixyshare.in

PixyShare is a modern, user-friendly platform designed to make photo sharing simple and beautiful. Built as a side project with passion and attention to detail, we provide a clean, elegant space where users can upload, share, and discover amazing photographs from around the world.

Our platform embraces the beauty of simplicity while offering powerful features. Whether you're a professional photographer looking to showcase your work, or someone who simply loves capturing moments, PixyShare provides the perfect environment to share your visual stories with a community that appreciates great imagery.

We believe that every photo tells a story, and our mission is to provide a platform where these stories can be shared, discovered, and appreciated by others. With our sleek glassmorphism design and intuitive interface, uploading and browsing photos becomes an enjoyable experience rather than a chore.

How Does PixyShare Work?

PixyShare operates on a simple yet powerful principle: making photo sharing accessible to everyone. Here's how our platform works:

Sign Up & Login: Create your account with just your email and basic information. Our secure authentication system ensures your account remains protected while keeping the registration process quick and straightforward.

Upload Images: Once logged in, you can easily upload your photos through our drag-and-drop interface. We support popular image formats (JPEG, JPG, PNG) and provide instant previews so you know exactly how your image will look before sharing.

Image Storage: Your images are securely stored using ImgBB's reliable cloud storage service, ensuring fast loading times and 99.9% uptime. This means your photos are always accessible and load quickly for viewers around the world.

Browse & Download: Anyone can browse the public gallery without needing an account. Users can discover amazing photos uploaded by our community and download images they love, making it easy to find inspiration or simply enjoy beautiful photography.

Social Integration: Add your Instagram username to your uploads so people can find and follow your work on other platforms, helping you build your photography presence across multiple channels.

How Do We Handle Your Email ID?

Your privacy and data security are our top priorities. We want to be completely transparent about how we handle your email address and personal information:

Purpose: We collect your email address solely for account creation and authentication purposes. This allows you to securely log in to your account and manage your uploads. We do not use your email for marketing, promotions, or any commercial purposes.

Storage & Security: Your email and account information are securely stored using Google Firebase's industry-standard security protocols. Firebase employs bank-level encryption and security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access.

No Spam Policy: We respect your inbox. PixyShare will never send you promotional emails, newsletters, or spam. The only communication you might receive is essential account-related information, such as password reset requests that you initiate.

Data Sharing: We never sell, rent, or share your email address with third parties, advertisers, or marketing companies. Your email remains private and is used exclusively for your PixyShare account functionality.

Account Control: You have full control over your account and data. If you ever decide to delete your account, all associated personal information, including your email address, will be permanently removed from our systems.

Transparency: We believe in being open about our data practices. If you have any questions about how we handle your information, feel free to reach out to us through our contact channels.
